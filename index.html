About TechOlympics

TechOlympics 2025: Inspire is Cincinnati’s premiere technology competition and expo, bringing students from across the Greater Cincinnati Area together to spend two days competing with other schools, networking, and learning more about the IT opportunities in Cincinnati. Students can compete in challenges ranging from programming to design to networking or attend workshops teaching valuable programming and design skills. You’ll also be able to get hands-on assistance with creating a professional resume and learning how to conduct yourself during a formal job interview. You’ll have direct access to Cincinnati’s best employers, many of whom will be looking for summer interns and future co-ops.

Registration for TechOlympics 2025 will be opening soon! Keep an eye on this space for more information about the upcoming event.

TechOlympics 2025 will be held in person this February 15-16th at Procter and Gamble Global Headquarters in Cincinnati!

Compete at TechOlympics

Compete against other schools in programming, networking, digital design, and more!

TechOlympics features a variety of competitions for students at every skill level.

TechOlympics Competitions

  • Wiki Races

    Navigate through Wikipedia quickly to find connections between topics.

  • Cybersecurity CTF

    Get hands-on experience with Python programming, hacking, and cybersecurity in a fun, capture the flag challenge!

  • TechOlympics Logo Design Competition

    In this competition, you get the chance to design the logo for next year's TechOlympics!

  • Speed Typing

    Test your typing speed against competitors in this simple yet thrilling competition.

  • TedXINTERalliance

    You will have up to 3 minutes to share your big idea in front of a few members of the TEDxCincinnati Team. You can do your talk on any idea worth sharing.

  • TechOlympics Logo Design Competition

    Shoot and edit a TechOlympics promo video in this exciting competition!

  • Speed Texting

    Test your texting speed against competitors in this simple yet thrilling competition.

Showcase Presentations

Represent your school in the TechOlympics Showcase, bringing with you an IT project you created with your school’s team.
